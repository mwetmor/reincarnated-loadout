Tidal Shadowmark Wardens — clean lineage cluster held by mechanical gate

Cluster 3 has the cleanest lineage signature (european × medieval × shadow+water 50/50; n=2) but both members failed mechanical pass (gauntlet_pass_rate 0.11; defensive cohort). Cohesion gate passed; joint-gate held. Substrate produced a clean faction identity that mechanically can't ship — see defensive-cohort callout below for three-gap diagnosis.

Defensive cohort 0% ship rate — three compositional gaps

All 11 held kits are defensive cohort (0 damage, 0 hybrid held). Refined diagnosis composes three separable architectural gaps surfacing as one mechanical failure: (1) Cohort weight mismatch — generating ~25-33% defensive vs ~5% genre empirical (legolas corpus); (2) Missing canonical defensive-viable mechanism (PoE thorns / Gladiator reflect / D4 Bash-thorns aspect) — our T4 strategy set lacks DEFENSE_AS_DAMAGE so defensive-shaped kits have no path to convert survival into damage; (3) Encounter battery (W-α6 24-cell ENCOUNTER_COHORT_KPM_BAND) lacks defensive-friendly variants that reward thorns/reflect/attrition specialty. gauntlet_pass_rate is composite (win × in-band × system-gate), not raw win rate — defensive kits may be winning fights but failing in-band cohort fit. KR routing recommended to rocket: split GPR into tier_1_outcome × in_band × sg_overall sub-metrics to diagnose which gap dominates.

gauntlet_pass_rate ≠ win_rate (terminology clarification)

gauntlet_pass_rate is the Discipline #26 PLAYABLE-AND-IN-BAND composite criterion: requires win AND in-cohort-KPM-band AND survival AND no sg_overall: BLOCK from ceiling-artifact gate. In sample sim data: 89.8% raw tier_1_outcome PROVISIONAL_PASS (wins) but only 11.5% in_band; the 78% gap between "won" and "won-correctly-for-cohort" is where defensive-cohort kits typically fail. Raw win rate not directly emitted to Phase 7 verdict log — composite GPR is the load-bearing metric.

Mega-cluster vs distinctive cluster trade-off

Cluster 1 has 22 of 33 archive kits — substrate-dominant but generic. Cluster 3 has 2 of 33 — substrate-distinctive but small. PM-1 clustering produces this bimodal distribution; cluster-cardinality variance is substrate-elected per Wanderer architecture principle (Amendment 1).

Substrate-led discipline empirically visible

53 distinct substrate weapons from 89K library used in 54 kits — near-zero substrate duplication. Cultural lineage spans 5 distinct traditions. Period spans pre-classical through industrial. Substrate vote at Phase 2 is honest to the catalogue's diversity.
