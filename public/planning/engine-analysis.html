1. Executive summary — top 7 actionable findings §

Amendment 2026-05-29 late — build-defining research findings landed A second research commission (legolas Mode A; 31 mechanisms × 9 ARPG games; sourced at agentic_orchestration/research/arpg-build-defining-mechanisms-2026-05-29/) substantially refines this doc's framing. The original findings below remain valid for axis-expansion + T4 strategy candidates. But the genre research reveals T4 is one of 7 mechanism families, not the singular surface for build-defining moments. Family B (extractable/imbue-able power — D2 Runewords, D3 Kanai's Cube, D4 Legendary Aspects) is the DOMINANT genre family by primary-crystallization moments and is ABSENT from Reincarnated's current architecture. See § 18–§ 21 for the full verdict + amendments. Verdict in brief: T4 architecture is preserved and is genuinely novel — but is insufficient alone. Family B is the highest-leverage Cycle 15+ architectural addition, surfacing as a design call BEFORE the T4 strategy set expansion proposed in § 11.
  1. Three axis classes exist, our engine canonicalizes only one fully. Mechanical (BC 8-axis + damage-scaling + cohort) is canonical and load-bearing. Experiential (Bossing/Speedfarm/Push/Generalist/Leveling/LeagueStart + Investment Tier + Playstyle Geometry) is empirically validated at 91% Skill+Class spine + 4 game-source breadth, ready for canonical promotion. Player-facing (the "feel" lexicon: Gear Dependent, Buff Management, Don't Get Hit, Difficult to Play Optimally, etc.) is unmeasured and ungoverned — community names it; we don't model it.
  2. The composite-vs-single-axis result is the most architecturally consequential finding of the sprint. Game determines composite-restriction pattern at Cramér's V=0.74 (bias-corrected; q_BH=3e-4 surviving Bonferroni). Causal mechanism: multiplicative loot substrate layer count (PoE 6 layers → 100% composite-required; LE 3 layers → 100% single-axis-viable). This is a Layer 1.5 architectural variable Reincarnated must explicitly choose. Recommendation: ≤3 layers, LE-style.
  3. The Skill+Class naming spine is universal (91%); the activity-suffix is Maxroll-D4-editorial-specific. Community vocabulary lives at [skill_identity] + [class_vestigial_reference] level. Build identity is not at the item/gear level (Apriori produced zero rules at min_support=0.10 — gear is too sparse and diverse). Implication: kit identity at our generation layer should match this spine — substrate-led skill identity + vestigial archetypal anchor.
  4. "Allround / Endgame Generalist" is the modal variant at 41% — larger than Push (22) or Speedfarm (9). The Speedfarm↔Push binary is real but the middle pole (Generalist) is the dominant player position. Variant cycling at our engine should reflect a trinary: Speedfarm / Generalist / Push, not just binary.
  5. The skill-role taxonomy is 7-fold and converges across games. Community organizes skill bars as Core (36) + Amp (73, 2-4 per build) + Mobility (21) + Defense (18) + Resource (8) + CC (7) + Grouping (1). Our flat-8-actives architecture has the budget; we don't enforce role-balance at generation. Doc 40 D82 (max-8-actives) preserved; role-distribution as kit-emergence vote candidate.
  6. Magic Find is a multi-temporal-scale mechanic, not a primary archetype. D2-legacy primary identity is dead. D4 retired explicit MF (Torment Tier replaces). PoE2 EA elevated to first-class explicit stat target (150% IIR endgame). Our engine has no MF analog; this is a deliberate architectural choice — the substrate signal is that the mechanic is either single-layer-difficulty-gate (D4) or 6-layer-cascade (PoE). We should pick a side or invent a third path.
  7. T4 capstones are the highest-leverage surface for absorbing the community vocabulary at the generative layer. The Layer 2 cycling slot in doc 47 § 4.6 (currently 6 strategies: 3 ELEMENT_CONVERSION + TRADE_OFF_REVERSED + GEOMETRY_COLLAPSE + RESOURCE_CONVERSION) is the natural locus for experiential-axis T4 expansion. This doc proposes 15 new candidate strategies, each anchored to community-validated experiential vocabulary, designed to compose with substrate-led discipline and Wanderer architecture.

6. Mechanical axis expansion candidates §

Two mechanical-axis expansions surface from the corpus that we currently underspecify:

6.1 Loot Substrate Layer Architecture (Layer 1.5 — coupling architecture)

Architectural variable + recommendation Reincarnated must explicitly choose its loot substrate layer count + coupling pattern at Cycle 15+ architectural design call. Per synthesis verdict + statistical analysis:
  • ≤3 multiplicative layers (LE-style simpler-multiplication) → preserves single-axis archetype viability; matches the recommendation
  • 3 layers with mid-multiplication (D4-style) → mid-restriction; viable but constrains
  • ≥5 layers with multiplicative cascade (PoE-style) → composite-required at endgame; player community pushback documented
Recommendation: Reincarnated commits to ≤3 multiplicative loot substrate layers with additive-or-simpler-multiplicative coupling.

6.2 Skill-Role Distribution as kit-emergence vote

Doc 40 D82 sets max 8 active skills per kit. Doc 47 has T4 routing. Neither enforces the skill-role distribution the community has converged on (Core + Amp + Mobility + Defense + Resource + CC). Adding role-distribution to Phase 3 quality vectors (q-vector) creates a substrate-led vote on whether a kit's mechanical composition resembles community-recognized "playable skill bar" patterns.

Proposed q-vector extension: q_role_balance = goodness-of-fit of kit's skill-role distribution against community-empirical role-distribution prior. Kits whose Phase 2 skill composition is degenerate (e.g., 6 Amp + 0 Core; or 4 Core + 0 Mobility) score low; community-empirical distributions score high. Pareto-2 archive selection inherits this.

8. Player-facing axis expansion candidates §

PF E. Gear Dependency Index (GDI)

Type: Continuous 0-1.
Definition: fraction of kit's effective DPS+survival that requires specific legendaries to manifest. GDI=0 means kit builds-from-base; GDI=1 means kit requires Mageblood-equivalent to function.
Computation: measured at gear-spec generation via per-kit damage-without-legendaries vs damage-with-legendaries ratio.
Source: "Gear Dependent" (n=9, top con) + "Needs Legendaries to Shine" (n=2).
T4 design rule: T4 capstones contribute to kit identity at GDI=0 (T4 fires at intrinsic level; legendaries amplify but don't gate).

PF F. Cognitive Load Index (CLI)

Type: Ordinal 1-5.
Definition: active maintenance burden of the kit. Counts: buff windows to track + cooldowns to manage + resource thresholds to maintain + position-conditional damage windows + trigger-condition awareness.
Computation: sum of D40 D55 triggered-passive count + active-skill count requiring sub-second timing + buff-condition gates.
Source: "Buff Management" / "Buff Upkeep" / "Cooldown Management" / "Buff Micromanagement" (n=5+ across markers).
T4 design rule: CLI≤3 kits get "easy to play" Playability rating; CLI≥4 kits should get "execution skill ceiling" reward (higher peak damage in skilled hands).

PF G. Execution Skill Floor / Ceiling

Type: Paired ordinal (floor 1-3 / ceiling 1-5).
Definition: floor = minimum skill required to execute the kit competently; ceiling = peak performance reward for skilled execution.
Source: "Easy to play" (floor=1) vs "Difficult to Play Optimally" (ceiling=5) vs "Demands multiple active buttons" (floor=3, ceiling=4).
T4 design rule: opportunity for skill-gated T4 capstones (Critical-Timing-Window T4 that rewards perfectly-timed execution with 2-3× damage spike).

PF H. Playstyle Geometry Tag

Type: Categorical 3-class (Ranged / Melee / All-Rounder).
Definition: discrete categorical derived from BC engagement_profile (close/medium/wide).
Source: PoE-Vault verbatim.
Computation: deterministic from BC: close → Melee; wide → Ranged; medium → All-Rounder.
Surface: loadout app categorical filter; player-facing tag in kit identity card.

PF I. Mobile-Damage Capability

Type: Boolean + magnitude.
Definition: kit can deal damage during movement (vs damage requires stand-still channel).
Source: "Deals Damage While Moving" pro (n=2); orthogonal to mobility skills (a mobility skill provides traversal; mobile-damage is the kit's ability to keep DPS active during traversal).
T4 design rule: distinctive T4 candidate — MOBILE_FORM T4 (kit's core skill auto-fires while moving).

PF J. Skill-Role Distribution Vector

Type: 7-vector (Core, Amp, Mobility, Defense, Resource, CC, Grouping counts).
Definition: distribution of kit's 8 active skill slots across community-empirical role taxonomy.
Computation: tag each emitted skill by role at Phase 2 generation; sum per kit.
Source: 7-fold convergent taxonomy across all 4 games.
Quality vector extension: q_role_balance = fitness against community-empirical prior; Pareto archive selection inherits.

10.2 The opportunity

The Layer 2 T4 cycling slot is the highest-leverage surface for absorbing the community-validated experiential + player-facing axes into the generative layer. 12. Algorithmic implementation — how axes drive T4 generation §

12.1 Two-phase T4 generation flow (proposed Cycle 15+)

PHASE A: Substrate-coordinate-driven T4 candidate enumeration
  for each kit k in Phase 4 archive:
    coord_k = (BC, lineage, cohort, faction_or_singleton,
               EXP_archetype_modal, EXP_investment_tier,
               PF_GDI, PF_CLI, PF_skill_ceil, PF_role_dist)
    eligible_T4s = []
    for each T4_strategy T in {6 current + 15 new = 21}:
      T_target_coord = T4_target_zone[T]
      distance_from_target = euclidean(coord_k, T_target_coord)
      eligibility_score = inverse(distance_from_target)
      eligible_T4s.append((T, eligibility_score))

PHASE B: Cohesion-judge-driven T4 selection
  for each kit k:
    rank eligible_T4s by eligibility_score
    apply experiential-archetype consistency filter:
      if kit's EXP_archetype_modal = "Bossing":
        boost BOSSING_MODE, PUSH_MODE, AMP_FUSION, SKILL_TIMING_WINDOW
        suppress SPEEDFARM_MODE, MOBILITY_AS_DAMAGE
      (etc per archetype)
    apply player-facing consistency filter:
      if kit's PF_GDI > 0.5:
        suppress GEAR_INDEPENDENT (kit fights its identity)
        boost SKILL_TIMING_WINDOW (high-GDI rewards skill)
      (etc per PF axis)
    select top-3 candidates; emit as kit's T4 set (Primary + Layer-2 + 3rd-slot)

22.5 Six refinements to the framework — for Pattern B re-engagement

  1. P4 weight may scale with acquisition friction. PoE 6-link feels canonical despite 2.5/5 score because thousand-fusing crafting struggle amplifies P4. Refinement candidate: P4 = 0 / 0.5 / 1 / 2 by acquisition friction tier.
  2. Build-defining is sometimes a COMPOSITION property, not a single-mechanism property. D4 single Aspect = 2.5/5; Aspect assembly = 5/5. Design implication for our Family B work.
  3. Sixth-property candidate: community legibility / shared-vocabulary anchor. "I play Enigma Hammerdin" communicates the build. Maybe downstream of P1+P5, maybe genuinely independent. Pattern B clarification needed.
  4. Different archetypes have different EXPECTED build-defining profiles. Push/Bossing kits expect 4–5; Generalist/Easy-to-play kits expect 0–2. The framework should accommodate per-archetype baseline.
  5. Mechanism family is the container; substrate properties are what matter. Family B mechanisms tend to score 4–5 because P4 is naturally present (extraction is memorable); Family D mechanisms max at 4 because P4 is naturally absent (allocation isn't memorable). We can engineer high-scoring mechanisms in any family if we design P1+P2+P3+P5 well and add P4 via acquisition-event design.

23.10 Post-materialization cosmetic personalization (Matt addendum 2026-05-29 late evening)

Architectural layer separation. Hidden-spirit-discovery (§ 23.1–23.9) operates at the mechanical-identity layer — gesture sequence resolves to BC + vector coordinate + substrate-led pre-generated character. The two layers are orthogonal; cosmetic personalization does NOT shift coordinates, does NOT alter substrate, does NOT change T4 or any mechanical property. Substrate-led discipline preserved at the mechanical layer; player creative agency operates at the cosmetic layer.
